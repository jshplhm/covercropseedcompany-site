Our Story

Cover crop expertise rooted in the Sacramento Valley and Sierra Foothills

Our leadership team brings over 10 years of global agronomy experience to the farms of Northern California. We work across the Sacramento Valley floor and up into the Sierra Foothills, from Yolo and Sutter counties through Nevada and Placer counties, with a deep understanding of the soils, climates, and cropping systems that define this region.

Based in Grass Valley, we work directly with growers to build cover crop programs that improve soil health, reduce inputs, and create long-term resilience on every operation we touch.

Cover Crop Management

Agronomic cover crop consulting across Nevada County, Placer County, and the Sacramento Valley

Cover cropping is a crop specific, farm specific process. A program that works in a Colusa County rice rotation looks nothing like one designed for a Nevada City vineyard or a Butte County almond orchard. We understand the full range of Northern California growing conditions and bring that regional knowledge to every consultation.

Please reach out to learn more about how cover crops can become a productive part of your production system.
